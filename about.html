Image to Text Converter

A simple online OCR webpage for converting images into editable text in just a few clicks.

What this service does

Image to Text Converter is a free online OCR service that allows you to convert image to text or image to Word-ready text that you can copy, edit, and reuse anywhere.

It is built to be simple: upload an image, choose a recognition language, run OCR, and get editable text directly in your browser.

Why we made it

Many OCR tools feel overloaded or require sign-up before you can do something basic. This page focuses on a lightweight experience for quick text extraction from screenshots, photos, scanned notes, receipts, and other image-based documents.
